Work Experience

Although I do not have not done any internships or work in any work spaces I have gone to field trips to see the environments of different work spaces.   One field trip I have gone to was Cisco in San Jose on February 5, 2018.   During this field trip we were able to see how they prevent intruders from hacking the systems,   and also got a tour of the facility. We also got to see how they use their technology to improve everyday problems   within large cities including parking, lighting, and security. I found this experience very important because I was   able to see how teams are able to work together on different projects.

I also found it very interesting on how he was able to multiple very different projects while still staying within the same career field.
